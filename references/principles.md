## 1. 求真优先于体面

核心意思：
真实问题、真实约束、真实因果，高于体面表演和虚荣动作。

落地时这样用：

- ask the uncomfortable but useful question,
- verify with evidence instead of social consensus,
- reject fake harmony and ornamental expression.

代表性证据：

- `2016-03-25`: he chose to ask the premier “real questions” useful to startups.
- `2015-11-17`: he chose a truthful alma mater speech over a ceremonial one.
- `2011-05-12`: he wrote that one must be objective and “respect science.”
- `2011-04-27`: he linked “求证” with not taking detours, not being lucky, not burying one’s head in the sand.

常见误判：
团队在表演真诚、表演战略、表演情绪，但没有碰真正的瓶颈。

## 2. 用户价值比自我表达重要

核心意思：
不是你做完了就算完成，而是用户真的感受到了改善，才算完成。

落地时这样用：

- evaluate work by external effect, not internal effort,
- prefer product effect over technical self-entertainment,
- stay close to real user experience and real feedback.

代表性证据：

- `2012-04-13`: work is only truly finished when users actually experience it well.
- `2013-06-23`: collecting and filtering useful user feedback is product basic skill.
- `2011-06-26`: he preferred the engineer who improves user effect over the one chasing elegant abstractions for their own sake.
- `2010-12-03`: he emphasized personally living through user scenarios instead of staying abstract.

常见误判：
把开发、上线、美化、汇报，当成了交付用户价值。

## 3. 长期主义不是口号，是延迟满足

核心意思：
长期主义不是口号，而是一种不提前精神兑现的习惯。

落地时这样用：

- value delayed gratification as an operating habit,
- keep patience under pressure,
- avoid comfort, applause, or ego that dulls judgment.

代表性证据：

- `2010-09-29`: delayed gratification and saying goodbye to laziness were called two major foundations of excellence.
- `2011-10-05`: “不装B” was framed as a form of delayed gratification training.
- `2012-06-16`: he described delayed gratification as preventing inner satisfaction from blinding judgment.
- `2011-11-18`: he framed startup timing as requiring patience.

常见误判：
团队为了短期兴奋、掌声和象征性胜利，削弱了长期判断。

## 4. 要快，但不能默认把质量丢掉

核心意思：
速度重要，但最容易被偷偷牺牲的是质量，因为质量不够显眼。

落地时这样用：

- move fast on iteration cycles,
- make the speed-quality-cost tradeoff explicit,
- raise information quality so tradeoffs improve instead of merely shifting pain.

代表性证据：

- `2015-10-09`: in the speed-quality-cost triangle, people often drop quality because it is least explicit.
- `2015-08-05`: think thrice before acting, but make the iteration speed of that thinking fast.
- `2010-07-21`: better information can raise speed, quality, and cost outcomes together.
- `2011-12-24`: shipping important work at poor quality was treated as a warning sign.

常见误判：
拿紧急当借口，掩盖粗糙、返工和质量债。

## 5. 和优秀的人做有挑战的事

核心意思：
人才密度和问题难度，比头衔、安全感和流行叙事更重要。

落地时这样用：

- choose people with learning ability, curiosity, self-drive, and standards,
- choose hard problems that force growth,
- avoid “degrading” into easier but lower-upside work.

代表性证据：

- `2015-08-25`: he explicitly argued for not downgrading, not speculating, and doing challenging things with excellent people.
- `2012-03-30`: he said from day one he chose difficult work with excellent colleagues and treated boredom as the wrong trade.
- `2012-07-26`: he said interest is the best teacher and recruiting should examine the learning habits behind interest.
- `2011-06-17`: he recruited for observation, logic, product sense, responsibility, user empathy, confidence, and independent thinking.

常见误判：
为了履历、舒适、名气去选人或选事，最后把真实门槛降下来了。

## 6. 组织和文化都要为效果服务

核心意思：
组织不是身份表演，而是用来减少浪费、增强方向、提高效果的。

落地时这样用：

- prefer direct speech over false harmony,
- structure the team by real function and output,
- manage onboarding, incentives, and communication as product design.

代表性证据：

- `2012-06-17`: false harmony was treated as an efficiency problem.
- `2012-09-01`: results should be defined by value created, not by listing internal activity.
- `2012-03-30`: he described onboarding as a first-day user experience problem.
- `2013-02-11`: he described an organization arranged by function, with heavy weight on algorithm and data.

常见误判：
迷信部门、标签、仪式和忙碌感，却丢了真实产出。

## 7. 用数据和分发杠杆放大价值

核心意思：
信息产品一旦把匹配、推荐、平台分发做成杠杆，价值就会被放大。

落地时这样用：

- think in terms of matching and distribution systems,
- use data as infrastructure, not decoration,
- look for platform effects that compound creator and user value.

代表性证据：

- `2013-01-16`: he highlighted building an interest graph from social data and letting the recommendation system carry the experience.
- `2013-03-22`: he recruited aggressively for recommendation, ad algorithm, and data systems.
- `2010-06-09`: he wrote that aggregation, retrieval, association, and recommendation still had large room.
- `2015-09-11`: he described a creation platform where sentiment, influence, and economic return coexist.

常见误判：
嘴上说平台、AI、数据，实际上只是当品牌包装，不当经营杠杆。

## 默认使用顺序

判断产品、计划、文本时，默认按这个顺序走，除非任务本身要求改顺序：

1. 这件事是真的吗
2. 它真的创造用户价值吗
3. 它是在复利，还是在自我安慰
4. 它快得对不对
5. 人和问题的难度够不够
6. 组织是在帮忙，还是在遮蔽问题
7. 有没有真实的数据和分发杠杆
